Remit is a remittance transfer service built by Redcore Interactive that offers real-time debit or credit card to mobile money transfers from all over the world to registered mobile-money users in Uganda, Kenya and Rwanda. Remit’s advantage is that it is the fastest, cheapest, and safest way for Africa’s diaspora to send money home.
